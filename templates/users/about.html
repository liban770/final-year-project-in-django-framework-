What is FYPMS?

ThesisTrack Management System (TTms) is a comprehensive web-based platform designed to streamline the management and tracking of final year projects for educational institutions. Our system provides a centralized hub where students, supervisors, and administrators can collaborate effectively throughout the project lifecycle.

Built with modern web technologies, TTms offers an intuitive interface that simplifies project submission, progress tracking, feedback management, and administrative oversight.

Our Purpose

For Students

Help students manage their final year projects more effectively by providing tools to:

  • Submit project proposals and track approval status
  • Upload project chapters and documents
  • Receive timely feedback from supervisors
  • Monitor project progress and deadlines

For Supervisors

Empower supervisors to effectively guide students by providing:

  • Easy access to assigned students' work
  • Streamlined feedback and approval process
  • Progress tracking and reporting tools
  • Efficient communication channels
